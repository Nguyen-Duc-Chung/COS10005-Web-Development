Sweet Life

Welcome to Sweet Life, a delightful escape where ice cream dreams come true! Since opening, Sweet Life has been dedicated to crafting high-quality, flavorful ice creams that bring joy to every scoop.

Our History

1.

Sweet Life was founded with a passion for crafting delicious, artisanal ice creams that bring people together. Established in 1950, it started as a small family-owned shop and quickly became a local favorite for its unique flavors and commitment to quality. Using only the finest ingredients, Sweet Life has expanded its menu over the years, offering a wide variety of flavors, including seasonal specials and customer-inspired creations.

Our Location

2.

Sweet Life is located in the heart of Portello, Milan, in the Metropolitan City of Milan, Italy. Nestled within this vibrant neighborhood, the shop is surrounded by charming streets and popular local spots, making it a favorite destination for both locals and visitors looking to enjoy artisanal ice creams in a cozy, inviting atmosphere.
